Introduction

Environmental laws are essential frameworks put in place to safeguard the natural environment. They regulate human activities to minimize harm to ecosystems, biodiversity, and public health.

Categories of Environmental Laws

Environmental laws cover diverse areas, including:

  • Air Quality Regulation
  • Water Protection
  • Waste Management
  • Conservation and Wildlife Protection
  • Climate Change Mitigation

Importance of Environmental Laws

These laws help reduce pollution, protect natural habitats, and ensure sustainable use of resources, contributing to the health and safety of both people and the environment.

Examples of Environmental Laws

  • Clean Air Act
  • Water Quality Act
  • Endangered Species Act
  • Resource Conservation and Recovery Act
  • Kyoto Protocol
